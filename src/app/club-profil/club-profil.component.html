📘 About

Juventus Football Club, based in Turin, Italy, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world. Founded in 1897, the club is known for its strong heritage, black-and-white stripes, and a tradition of excellence both in Italy and across Europe. Juventus has won numerous Serie A titles and international trophies, and continues to build a legacy of performance, professionalism, and passion.
